Our Technology

Built for the real world

Three pillars that make Ventra Edge fundamentally different from legacy systems.

Privacy-First Sensing

Wi-Fi Channel State Information (CSI) detects occupancy patterns without any cameras or microphones — fully compliant and non-intrusive.

Edge AI Resilience

Lightweight ML models run directly on ESP32 microcontrollers. No cloud dependency — the system keeps working even during network outages.

Predictive Control

Moves beyond rule-based timers to a feedforward architecture that dynamically adjusts VAV dampers and VFD fans based on real occupancy.

We drive intelligent energy management through privacy-first occupancy sensing and on-device machine learning — ensuring efficient, adaptive HVAC control for every office space.

Area Description

This zone is monitored by multiple ESP32 nodes using Wi-Fi CSI (Channel State Information) to detect occupancy without cameras. When occupancy is detected, the Edge AI model dynamically increases the VAV damper angle and VFD fan speed to maintain air quality. When the area is empty, both are reduced to conserve energy — directly cutting HVAC waste.
